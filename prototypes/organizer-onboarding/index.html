Step 3 of 6 — Booking setup
How do you want to handle registrations?
You can list your races for free with a link to your own system, or enable direct booking on RaceFinder. You can change this per-race at any time.
Registration model
🔗
External link
RaceFinder shows your race and links athletes to your existing registration page. Free forever.
⚡
Direct booking on RaceFinder
Athletes register and pay without leaving RaceFinder. 5% platform fee per booking. Pro plan required.
ℹ You can mix both models — keep some races external and enable direct booking on your flagship events. No migration required.

Step 4 of 6 — Payouts
Where should we send your money?
Payouts are processed via SEPA bank transfer 14 days after each race ends. Add your bank account now so there are no delays when your first race closes.
🔒 Bank details are encrypted and stored securely. RaceFinder never stores card numbers. Payouts go through a licensed payment processor.
